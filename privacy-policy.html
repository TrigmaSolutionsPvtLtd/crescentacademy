Privacy Policy

Updated on January 1st, 2017

PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”), effective January 1, 2017, applies to all information collected through the website (“Site”) of Crescent Academies LLC d.b.a. Crescent Academy (“Crescent”), and/or collected by Crescent through through mail, telephone, email, in person, or otherwise. By using this Site, enrolling in courses or otherwise doing business with or interacting with Crescent in circumstances covered in this Policy, you are accepting the practices described in this Policy and you expressly consent to the application of this Policy to the collection, storage, use and disclosure of all your personal information as described. We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time, and will we post updated or revised policies on our website, CrescentAcademy.com.

If you have any questions or concerns in regards to this policy, or want further information, please feel free to contact Crescent at Privacy@CrescentAcademy.com.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect and use the following types of personal information upon your inquiry and application for registration for a Crescent offering: your first and last name; date of birth; schooling history and learning limitations; language proficiency; allergies and medical provider contact; birthplace; parental contact information; home environment details, including primary language spoken and household makeup; local and permanent mailing addresses; mailing address at the time you enrolled; email address; date enrollment agreement was signed; courses you’ve enrolled in and associated costs; third party payer identifying information; total institutional charges charged and paid; information regarding your use of the Site; information that your browser sends whenever you visit the Site; and any other information you provide to us. You do not need to register or provide us with any personal information in order to simply visit and view the Site.

Crescent also automatically collects your IP address and referring website address, browser type, domain name and website access frequency. This information is intended to help facilitate your online experience on the Site. Crescent uses cookies to track usage patterns on the Site, as well as to offer other functionalities. You can turn off cookies, but that will affect your online experience. See below for more detail on cookies.

Crescent stores any comments or posts on the Site from Users, as well as any direct messages from Users to Crescent.

You may at any time request to see and update or correct the information you have provided to us by visiting your student dashboard, or emailing us at Privacy@CrescentAcademy.com. You may also write to us at PO Box 54545, Irvine, CA 92619.

DO NOT TRACK DISCLOSURE

Our Site does not follow the Do Not Track setting.

USE OF INFORMATION

Subject to all applicable court orders, laws, rules and regulations, Crescent reserves the right to use your personal information for any purposes that Crescent may choose, including sharing such information with third parties for purposes such as hosting and maintenance of the website, database storage and management, advertising sales, email marketing, e-commerce functions and credit card/payment processing. Current third party providers include GoDaddy for website hosting and database management, Crescent Applications for website maintenance, and Paypal for payment processing.

Crescent also uses Personal Information so that we may supply specific information requested by students, manage enrollments, and send students timely information. Crescent uses the automatically collected information to administer our offerings services, fulfill enrollment requests, and to optimize the Site navigation experience.

COOKIES

Your web browser offers so-called “cookies” which, if you allow their use, store small amounts of data on your computer when you visit a website. Cookies do not contain any Personal Information about you and therefore cannot be used to identify you personally. However, cookies enable us to customize our content according to your preferences. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by modifying your browser settings. Our Sites are viewable even if you disable the cookie function on your browser. However, if you disable cookies, some advanced and customized features will not be available to you.

LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

Our Site may link to other Sites . Linking is not an endorsement of such sites, and Crescent does not retain any control of such websites and is not responsible for the privacy practices or contents of such websites. Visitors should refer to those sites’ privacy policies for their information.

OUR POLICY TOWARDS CHILDREN

We will not knowingly collect Personal Information via the Sites from visitors younger than thirteen (13) years of age. We encourage parents to talk to their children about their use of the Internet and the information they disclose on websites. We may offer services for persons under 18 years old, but these services are to be purchased by adults. If you are under 18 years of age, you must have the involvement and consent of a parent or guardian. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal identifiable Information, we will delete it from our files. If any parent or guardian becomes aware that their child under the age of 13 has provided us with personally identifiable information without their consent (or that we have otherwise collected or obtained such information), please contact us at Privacy@CrescentAcademy.com.

CHANGE IN OWNERSHIP

In the event we change ownership or merge with another entity, we reserve the right to transfer, sell, or assign all information that we have collected.
