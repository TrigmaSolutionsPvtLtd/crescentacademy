About Crescent Academy

Crescent Academy is Orange County's after-school learning center for
Arabic, Islamic Studies & Quran!

Purpose

To supplement public education with the ethnic and religious education needed for Muslims in America.

Vision

To become the best after school learning center, in America, for Arabic, Islamic Studies & Quran, by 2020.

Mission

To teach young Muslims Arabic, Islamic Studies & Quran in order to prepare them for the future in a manner consistent with the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah.

Ahmad El Gamal

President

Imam Ahmad El Gamal was born in Egypt in 1978 and immigrated to the United States in 1994. He is now an entrepreneur with a passion for religion and interfaith activities. Through an online program, he studies at the faculty of Islamic Sciences at Al-Azhar University in Egypt. Imam Ahmad holds a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the University of Colorado at Boulder and a Certificate in Islamic Leadership from Bayan Claremont. He currently serves as the Chairman of Crescent Food Bank and leads the new Muslim support group at the Islamic Center of Irvine. His experience in Southern California includes serving on the board of UPLIFT Charity and on the outreach committee at the Islamic Center of Irvine. His experience in Egypt includes cofounding two elementary schools and serving on the board of the Egyptian Food Bank. He travels back and forth between Egypt and the U.S. quite frequently to take care of his businesses and responsibilities in both countries. Imam Ahmad is married and has four children.

Our Story

Crescent Academy was established in Irvine, California to provide Muslims in America with the ethnic and religious education that they need. Public schools offer excellent secular education, and private Islamic schools are too expensive. Crescent Academy focuses on what is needed to supplement the already excellent education provided by Public schools and funded by our tax dollars.

The Best of Both Worlds

Public schools do an excellent job teaching english, math, science, etc. That's paid for using your tax dollars. Why pay again for a full-day Islamic school, when you can just pay a fraction of that cost for the supplement that your child needs?

No Prior Knowledge Required

We understand that your child may be a beginner, even in High School, so we adapt our teaching accordingly.

2 Hours Twice a Week

Many parents complain that weekend school is inconvenient and is not enough. That is why our classes are two hours each, twice a week, on school days.

Character Building

Islam is not only a set of rituals, it is a complete lifestyle. Crescent Academy follows the prophetic model in building a comprehensive character.

Skilled Teachers

One has to select carefully who they take their religion from. Our teachers are skilled role models who are trained on how to deliver Islam the correct way.

Inquiry-Based Approach

Students learn better when they ask for it! Our teaching approach is to let the students ask first in order to be interested in the answer.

Convenient Location

Crescent Academy is conveniently located in the heart of Orange County close to the intersection of I-5 and CA-405 in the city of Irvine.
